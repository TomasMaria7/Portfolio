Expertise

I focus on Delveloping programs.

Throughout my journey, I have acquired skills in various programming languages and used simulators to apply these competencies in small assignments and projects. I have experience with Kotlin, Java, C, and C++, and have utilized circuit simulators such as LogiSim, MultiSim, and MPLAB.

  • Kotlin

    It was with Kotlin that I delved into a deep study for the first time, marking the beginning of serious work and providing me with the necessary learning to develop various programs. This language also served as a gateway for exploring other languages. One notable project I accomplished with Kotlin was the development of the "Jogo das Tendas".

  • Java

    On that occasion, I had the opportunity to work with another programming language in the "Rock in Deisi" project, where I could further deepen my programming skills and apply the fundamentals I had acquired with Kotlin.

  • C

    Building upon the foundations of Kotlin, I had the opportunity to enhance my knowledge in another programming language. Despite the differences compared to Kotlin, with dedication and study, I adapted to this language. Undoubtedly, it was with the C language that I significantly deepened my programming knowledge, learning different concepts and techniques than those I had explored in Kotlin.

  • C++

    In my 12th year, I had my first experience with programming, where I dedicated a semester to developing some programs in C++. However, due to the limited duration of that period, I did not have the opportunity to explore the language in its entirety. As a result, I have relatively less experience working with C++ compared to other languages.

  • MultiSiM

    It was with great enthusiasm that I encountered the first circuit simulator I used, through which I completed a project involving the development of an adder calculator. During this process, I had the opportunity to explore the extensive capabilities of this simulator and invested a significant amount of time working with it, enhancing my knowledge and skills.

  • LogiSIM

    Although I used the simulator less frequently than Logisim, I also had the opportunity to employ this simulator in specific projects. A notable example was the design project of an irrigation system for a garden, which involved integrating various sensors and timers. Despite having distinct characteristics compared to Logisim, I felt quite comfortable working with this simulator and made the most of its functionalities.

  • MPLAB

    The simulator in which I have the least experience, without a doubt, was the one I used to complete the irrigation project for the garden. Although it was a less frequent experience, I dedicated myself to learning and using the simulator specifically for this project, aiming to achieve the desired objectives.
